Privacy Policy

How AutoFit Pro handles your store's data

AutoFit Pro is a vehicle fitment management app for Shopify. This policy explains, in plain terms, exactly what data we sync from your store, why we sync it, and what we do — and don't — keep.

No customer data collected Product & fitment data only Auto-deleted on uninstall GDPR webhook compliant
Data flow at a glance

Your Shopify Store

Products you add, edit, or remove. Fitment fields and values you configure in the AutoFit Pro admin.

AutoFit Pro Database

Stores fitment definitions and references to your products as tags, kept synchronized with your store through Shopify APIs and webhooks.

Your Storefront

The app embed displays your fitment fields on the storefront, allowing shoppers to select vehicle details and view products based on the configured fitment data.

We never collect, store, or process your customers' personal information — no names, emails, addresses, or order data ever reach our database.

01

Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to AutoFit Pro (the "App"), built and operated by Md. Al Amin Islam ("we," "us," "our"), available on the Shopify App Store. It describes how we handle data when a merchant ("you," "your store") installs the App on a Shopify store.

This policy should be read alongside Shopify's own Privacy Policy, which governs Shopify's handling of merchant and customer data independently of this App.

02

Information we collect

AutoFit Pro only reads and stores store-level and product-level data that is necessary for fitment management to work. Specifically:

Data type What it includes When it's synced
Fitment field definitions The custom fields you create in the AutoFit Pro admin (e.g. Year, Make, Model, Trim) and the values you assign to them. When you create, edit, or delete a fitment field
Product references Product ID, title, handle, and the tags generated from your fitment data. When a product is added, updated, or removed in your store
Shop information Shop domain, app installation status, and plan/billing status required to operate the App. On install, and when billing status changes

We do not collect data beyond what is required to generate fitment tags and power the storefront search widget described above.

03

Information we don't collect

AutoFit Pro is built to operate entirely on store and product data. We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Customer names, emails, phone numbers, or shipping addresses
  • Order, checkout, or payment information
  • Customer browsing behavior or analytics
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII) about your shoppers
Why this matters Because the App never touches customer-level data, there is no customer data to secure, retain, or delete — it simply never enters our systems in the first place.
04

How we use this information

  • Field management — to let you create, edit, and organize dynamic fitment fields from the App admin.
  • Tag generation — to convert the fitment data you assign to a product into Shopify product tags.
  • Storefront search — to power the app embed block on your storefront, which displays your fitment fields, allows shoppers to select vehicle details, and shows products based on the configured fitment information.
  • Store-product sync — to keep fitment data accurate whenever you add, modify, or remove a product in your store.
05

Syncing & updates

AutoFit Pro listens for product activity in your store (create, update, delete) via Shopify's APIs and webhooks, and reflects those changes in our database automatically. In practice this means:

  • Adding a new product makes it available for fitment tagging right away.
  • Editing a product's fitment data updates the associated tags without manual re-syncing.
  • Deleting a product removes its associated fitment data and references from our database.
06

Storage & security

Fitment field definitions and product references are stored in a secure database infrastructure managed by AutoFit Pro. Access is restricted to authorized systems and personnel. All data is protected during transmission using TLS encryption, and database access is limited to systems required for the operation, maintenance, and support of the App.

07

Retention & deletion

While the App is installed

Your store's fitment and product reference data is retained for as long as the App remains installed, so that fitment search continues to function correctly on your storefront.

When the App is uninstalled

When you uninstall AutoFit Pro, all data associated with your store — fitment field definitions, product references, and shop information — is automatically and permanently removed from our database.

When a store is deactivated or closed

If your store is deactivated, closed, or removed from Shopify, Shopify notifies the App via the mandatory shop/redact webhook, and we delete the corresponding store data from our systems automatically — no manual request is needed.

08

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your store's data. We share data only with the limited service providers needed to run the App, such as:

  • Our hosting and database infrastructure providers, Railway (hosting platform) and Neon PostgreSQL (database service)
  • Shopify, via its standard APIs, to read and write product and tag data on your behalf

These providers process data solely to deliver App functionality and are bound by their own confidentiality and security obligations.

09

Cookies on your storefront

The AutoFit Pro app embed renders the fitment search widget on your storefront. It does not set tracking or advertising cookies, and does not collect shopper identifiers. Any local storage used is limited to remembering a shopper's in-session fitment selections, purely to improve the search experience.

10

Your rights & GDPR compliance

Although AutoFit Pro does not collect customer personal data, we comply with Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhooks as part of the Shopify App Store requirements:

  • customers/data_request — acknowledged; since we hold no customer data, there is nothing to return.
  • customers/redact — acknowledged; since we hold no customer data, there is nothing to redact.
  • shop/redact — triggers deletion of all store-level data described in this policy.

As a merchant, you may request a summary or deletion of the store data we hold at any time by contacting us using the details below.

11

Children's privacy

AutoFit Pro is a business-to-business tool intended for use by Shopify merchants and store administrators. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data relating to children through the App.
